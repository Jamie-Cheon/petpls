Our Story

There once was a lady who dreams to have a pet supply store which could carry the best products to all the pet lovers.

She met a man who is a big cat lover and wants to start a cat cafe that serves great coffee and pastries. Then they fell in love and decided to gather up their ideas to create a place where full of joy and happiness.

In Petpls, we are hoping that you would have a memorable and fantastic experience. We are also hoping that together, we can create a better life and environment for our pets.
